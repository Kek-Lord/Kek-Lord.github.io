French Toast Recipe

This classic French toast recipe is a simple and delicious breakfast option. Follow the easy steps below to make tasty French toast at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread (white, whole wheat, or any preferred type)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Maple syrup, powdered sugar, or toppings of your choice

Instructions

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla extract (if using), and ground cinnamon (if using) until well combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and add a little butter or oil to coat the surface.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, ensuring both sides are coated evenly.
  4. Place the coated bread slices onto the preheated skillet or griddle.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Transfer the cooked French toast onto a plate.
  7. Serve hot with maple syrup, powdered sugar, or your favorite toppings.
  8. Enjoy your delicious homemade French toast!
