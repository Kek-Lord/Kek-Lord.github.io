Omelette Recipe

This classic omelette recipe is perfect for a quick and satisfying breakfast. Follow the simple steps below to make a delicious omelette.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Fillings of your choice (e.g., diced vegetables, cooked meats)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook undisturbed for a minute.
  4. Using a spatula, gently push the cooked edges of the omelette towards the center, allowing the uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  5. Once the omelette is mostly set but still slightly runny on top, sprinkle grated cheese (if using) and add your desired fillings onto one half of the omelette.
  6. Using the spatula, carefully fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings.
  7. Cook for another minute until the cheese melts and the omelette is cooked through.
  8. Slide the omelette onto a plate, slice if desired, and serve hot.
