Scrambled Eggs Recipe

This classic scrambled eggs recipe is a quick and nutritious breakfast option. Follow the simple steps below to make delicious scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook undisturbed for a minute.
  4. Using a spatula, gently push the cooked edges of the eggs towards the center, allowing the uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  5. Continue cooking and gently stirring until the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny.
  6. Remove from heat and let the residual heat finish cooking the eggs to your desired consistency.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
