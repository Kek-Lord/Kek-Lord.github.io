Classic Spaghetti Recipe

This classic spaghetti recipe is perfect for a quick, nutritious lunch that's packed with flavor. Ideal for students or anyone looking for a satisfying meal. Follow the simple steps below to enjoy this beloved dish.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 400g canned whole tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano or basil
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. Fill a large pot with water, add salt, and bring to a boil. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic and onion, and saut√© until soft.
  3. Add tomatoes, sugar, salt, pepper, and herbs to the pan and simmer.
  4. Combine spaghetti with sauce, adjust seasoning, and add pasta water if needed.
  5. Serve garnished with basil leaves and Parmesan cheese.
